## Hi, I'm [Your Name]

I'm a [computational biologist / grad student / researcher] working on [your area]. This blog is where I write about the things that excite me at the intersection of biology, computation, and society.

**Sequence Space** covers three kinds of writing:

- **Research** — I break down interesting papers and findings, trying to make them accessible to someone with a biology *or* CS background (but not necessarily both).
- **Tutorials** — Practical coding guides for comp bio tasks. Expect Python, R, and the occasional Nextflow pipeline.
- **Commentary** — Slower, more opinionated writing about how our field relates to the world: ethics, policy, the sociology of science, and what it means that we can now sequence a genome for a few hundred dollars.

I'm not trying to be exhaustive or authoritative — just genuinely curious and willing to think in public.
