About the Blog

Posts on this blog fall into a few categories: deep dives into methods and tools I use regularly, breakdowns of papers I find important or interesting, tutorials, and occasional takes on where the field is heading. I try to include working code wherever it helps.

I don't have comments enabled because I don't have time to moderate them — but I genuinely love hearing from readers. Email is the best way to reach me, and I try to respond to everything.
